Metrics Guide
Scoring Modes
Full SE Generates 5 diverse responses, clusters them by semantic similarity, then computes energy flow across clusters using sum-normalized Boltzmann logits. The main answer's cluster energy becomes the confidence score. Most thorough but slowest (~60-120s).
Fast SLT Generates 1 response, runs a separate forward pass, then extracts the second-to-last token's (SLT) hidden states. Two trained logistic regression probes (energy + entropy) predict hallucination risk from these hidden states. Post-generation signal (~5-15s).
Fast TBG Two-phase mode. Phase 1: analyzes the last prompt token's (TBG) hidden states before generation for a pre-generation risk estimate. Phase 2: generates a response with SLT sentence-level analysis. Badge shows TBG score; sentences use SLT probes (~5-15s).
Overall Confidence Badge
Confidence % Computed as 1 - combined_risk. Thresholds: HIGH (≥65%), MEDIUM (35-65%), LOW (<35%). In Full SE mode this comes from the main cluster's energy. In probe modes it comes from the combined probe risk score.
Detail Metrics (Probe Modes: SLT / TBG)
Energy Risk Output of the energy probe: 1 - predict_proba(hidden_states)[class=1]. The probe was trained on binarized Semantic Energy teacher scores (from full multi-sample clustering). It predicts whether the model's internal state at this token resembles states that produced correct answers (low risk) or incorrect answers (high risk). Range: 0 (confident) to 1 (risky).
Entropy Risk Output of the entropy probe: predict_proba(hidden_states)[class=1]. Trained on binarized cluster-assignment entropy teacher scores. Captures whether the model's hidden representations show internal uncertainty (high entropy = model would produce diverse, inconsistent answers if sampled multiple times). Range: 0 (certain) to 1 (uncertain).
Combined Risk For short answers (≤100 tokens): (energy_risk + entropy_risk) / 2. For long answers (>100 tokens): a weighted blend of the overall SLT entropy risk (15%), the highest per-sentence probe risk (adaptive weight), and the mean per-sentence probe risk (remaining weight). This ensures long multi-sentence responses are scored by their most risky claims, not just the overall SLT signal.
Detail Metrics (Full SE Mode)
Clusters Found Number of semantically distinct answer groups from 5 generated responses. Fewer clusters = more consistent = higher confidence. 1 cluster means all 5 responses were semantically equivalent.
Cluster Energy Computed via sum_normalize(boltzmann_logits_per_cluster). Each cluster's energy represents its share of the total logit mass. The main answer's cluster energy is the confidence score. All cluster energies sum to 1.0.
Sentence-Level Analysis
Sentence Confidence Per-sentence logit-based score (B1 baseline). For each sentence, the mean chosen-token logit is mapped through a calibrated sigmoid: σ((logit - center) / scale). Measures how certain the model was about each word choice in that sentence. This is NOT Semantic Energy — no clustering is performed.
Sentence Probe Risk Per-sentence hallucination risk using two trained probes (SLT mode only). Hidden states at each sentence's last token are extracted and scored by both the energy probe and entropy probe. Combined via AUROC-weighted average: entropy 51.5% + energy 48.5%. When probe risk ≥0.65, the sentence is flagged as low confidence (overrides the logit-based level).
Sentence Avg Conf Mean of all per-sentence logit confidence scores (claim sentences only). A supplementary signal showing overall token-level certainty. High sentence avg conf with low probe confidence can indicate the model was "confidently wrong."
Highlighting Sentences are tinted in the response bubble. No tint = high confidence. Amber tint = medium confidence. Red tint = low confidence. Non-claim sentences (greetings, filler) are skipped and show a dash. Hover any sentence for exact scores.
