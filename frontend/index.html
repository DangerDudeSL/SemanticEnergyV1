Metrics Guide
Scoring Modes
Full SE Generates 5 diverse responses, clusters them by semantic similarity, then calculates energy flow across clusters. Most thorough but slowest (~60-120s).
Fast SLT Generates 1 response, then analyzes the second-to-last token's hidden states with a trained probe. Post-generation signal (~5-15s).
Fast TBG Analyzes the last prompt token's hidden states before any generation. Pre-generation risk estimate (~0.5-2s).
Overall Confidence
Confidence Badge The main hallucination score. In Full SE mode, this comes from cluster energy distribution. In SLT/TBG modes, it comes from trained probes on hidden states. Higher = more likely correct.
Detail Metrics (Probe Modes)
Energy Risk Risk score from the energy probe. Measures if the model's internal state resembles patterns seen in correct vs incorrect answers. Lower = more confident.
Entropy Risk Risk score from the entropy probe. Captures uncertainty in the model's hidden representations. Lower = less uncertain.
Combined Risk Average of energy and entropy risk. Used to determine the overall confidence level (High/Medium/Low).
Detail Metrics (Full SE)
Clusters Found Number of semantically distinct answer groups. Fewer clusters = more consistent responses = higher confidence.
Cluster Energy Normalized energy for each cluster from Fermi-Dirac logit flows. The main answer's cluster energy is the confidence score.
Sentence-Level Analysis
Sentence Confidence Per-sentence score based on the model's raw token logits, mapped through a sigmoid function. Measures how certain the model was about each word choice. Uses absolute thresholds, not relative comparison.
Sentence Probe Risk Per-sentence hallucination risk from two trained probes (SLT mode only). The energy probe (layers 17-21) detects if the hidden state matches correctness patterns. The entropy probe (layers 20-24) detects internal uncertainty. Both run on hidden states at each sentence's last token and are combined with AUROC-weighted averaging. When available, this overrides the logit-based level for highlighting.
Energy vs Entropy Risk Energy risk measures whether the model's internal state resembles patterns seen in correct answers (from Full SE clustering). Entropy risk measures internal uncertainty in hidden representations. Combined risk is their weighted average (entropy 51.5%, energy 48.5%, based on validation accuracy). Hover over sentences or analysis rows to see individual probe values.
Sentence Avg Conf Average of all per-sentence confidence scores. A supplementary signal showing overall logit-level certainty across the full response.
Highlighting Sentences are tinted in the response bubble. No tint = high confidence (OK). Amber tint = medium confidence (WARN). Red tint = low confidence (RISK). Hover for exact percentage.
