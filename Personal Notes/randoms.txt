2. What Does "RC" Mean?
RC = "Reading Comprehension". TriviaQA has two configs on HuggingFace:

rc — The full dataset with Wikipedia + search evidence documents (~650K triples). We only use the questions/answers, not the evidence passages (the LLM generates answers from parametric memory, not from provided context).
unfiltered — Larger, noisier version
The rc config is the standard one used in the original TriviaQA paper and in the Semantic Entropy paper. Keep using rc.
