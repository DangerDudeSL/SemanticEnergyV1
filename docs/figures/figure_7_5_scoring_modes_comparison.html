Full Semantic Energy /chat endpoint
1. Generate 5 Responses
Stochastic sampling, temp=0.7
Per-token logits & probs captured
2. B1 Sentence Scoring
pysbd split, claim filter
Sigmoid logit confidence per sentence
3. Semantic Clustering
Pairwise LLM equivalence check
Greedy agglomerative clustering
4. Energy & Entropy Computation
Boltzmann logits → cluster aggregate
Shannon entropy over cluster sizes
5. Hidden State Extraction
Not needed — uses full pipeline
6. Probe Scoring
Not needed — uses full pipeline
7. Confidence Mapping
energy_score > 0.80 → high
energy_score > 0.50 → medium
Output
answer + energy confidence score
+ sentence scores (B1 only)
+ cluster debug data
60 – 120 seconds
Up to 15 forward passes
Fast SLT (Post-Generation) /score_fast_slt endpoint
1. Generate 1 Response
Single generation, same config
Per-token logits & probs captured
2. B1 Sentence Scoring
pysbd split, claim filter
Sigmoid logit confidence per sentence
3. Semantic Clustering
Skipped — only 1 sample
4. Energy & Entropy Computation
Skipped — no clusters
5. Hidden State Extraction
Forward pass on (prompt + answer)
SLT + per-sentence hidden states
6. Probe Scoring
Overall: SLT energy + entropy probes
Per-sentence: dual-probe (AUROC-weighted)
Wentropy=0.515, Wenergy=0.485
7. Conditional Aggregation
Short (≤100 tok): SLT + mean blend
Long (>100 tok): weighted combination
risk < 0.35 → high confidence
Output
answer + combined risk score
+ per-sentence probe risks
+ B1 confidence + confidence level
5 – 15 seconds
2 forward passes (1 gen + 1 extraction)
Fast TBG (Pre-Generation) /score_fast_tbg endpoint
1. Generate Responses
Skipped — no generation needed
2. B1 Sentence Scoring
Skipped — no answer to score
3. Semantic Clustering
Skipped — no samples
4. Energy & Entropy Computation
Skipped — no clusters
5. Hidden State Extraction
Forward pass on prompt only
TBG hidden state at last prompt token
6. Probe Scoring
TBG energy probe → energy_risk
TBG entropy probe → entropy_risk
combined = (energy + entropy) / 2
7. Confidence Mapping
risk < 0.35 → high
risk < 0.65 → medium
risk ≥ 0.65 → low
Output
energy risk + entropy risk
+ combined risk
+ confidence level (no answer)
0.5 – 2 seconds
1 forward pass (prompt only)
Figure 7.5 — Comparison of the three scoring modes. Each column represents one API endpoint. Numbered steps correspond to the same processing stage; coloured left borders indicate active steps, while greyed-out steps with "SKIPPED" labels are not executed in that mode. Full Semantic Energy executes all pipeline stages (up to 15 forward passes, 60–120s). Fast SLT generates one answer and uses trained probes for risk estimation (2 forward passes, 5–15s). Fast TBG requires no generation at all — a single forward pass on the prompt produces a pre-generation risk score (0.5–2s).
